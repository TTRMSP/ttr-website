TTR MSP Frequently Asked Questions

What does Total Tech Resources do?

Total Tech Resources (TTR) offers managed IT provider services enabling partners to expand their growth affordably. As businesses reaches a stage requiring an internal IT team or a single technician for managing technology needs, we assist in realizing their vision of the broader picture. We aid partners in budgeting and overseeing technology infrastructure, ensuring stable growth trajectories for their businesses.

Why Choose Total Tech Resources MSP?

There are several compelling reasons why opting for a Managed Service Provider (MSP) over hiring in-house IT staff is advantageous, with cost being the primary factor. Consider the substantial savings achieved by paying a single monthly fee for access to a comprehensive support team available round the clock, as opposed to the significant expenses associated with recruiting and maintaining additional staff members, including the obligation of providing benefits. Moreover, engaging with a support company often comes with tax incentives, further enhancing the financial benefits of this approach.

Does Total Tech Resources MSP offer 24/7 support?

Yes, Not only does TTR provide round-the-clock, 365-day services, but we also specialize in tailoring our offerings to precisely meet your unique needs. Our commitment to customization ensures that you receive solutions that are perfectly aligned with your requirements, preferences, and objectives. Whether it's optimizing operational efficiency, enhancing customer experiences, or achieving specific business goals, TTR goes above and beyond to deliver results that exceed expectations. Our flexible approach and dedication to personalized service set us apart as a trusted partner in helping you achieve success.

Does Total Tech Resources MSP require a contract?

We offer a best-in-class contract unlike anything you've seen before. Our founder values transparency and accountability, so our contract serves as guidelines for both parties. It can be terminated at any time and for any reason without any negative impact on either party. We want you to choose us because we provide exceptional service, and we see no need to bind anyone into long-term commitments. Our focus is on delivering outstanding service.

Does Total Tech Resources Service Small business?

Yes, Total Tech Resources (TTR) does service small businesses. Our partnership philosophy emphasizes inclusivity and flexibility, ensuring that we cater to organizations of all sizes. We value the individual needs and objectives of each business we work with, prioritizing their success above financial gains. Our approach involves customizing services to address the unique challenges faced by small businesses, fostering meaningful relationships based on mutual respect and shared goals. Through this tailored approach, we aim to deliver tangible results that support the growth and prosperity of small businesses.
